## 🧠 Overview

**Factorio** is a large-scale automation and factory construction game focused on engineering, logistics, resource management, and industrial expansion. Players are tasked with building increasingly complex production systems while transforming a hostile alien planet into a fully automated industrial network.

Starting with basic mining and crafting, the game gradually evolves into massive interconnected factories powered by conveyor systems, robotic logistics, advanced manufacturing chains, and large-scale energy infrastructure. Every production line can be optimized, expanded, and redesigned to improve efficiency and maximize output.

Factorio emphasizes strategic planning and automation, encouraging players to develop sophisticated transport systems, optimize supply chains, and manage growing industrial operations across expansive maps. The deeper the progression becomes, the more intricate and rewarding the factory systems evolve.

The game also includes combat and defensive mechanics, requiring players to protect industrial infrastructure from hostile alien creatures attracted by environmental pollution and factory expansion. Automated defenses, military technology, and strategic base layouts become essential for survival as operations scale upward.

Its highly optimized simulation engine allows enormous factories with thousands of moving components to run smoothly while supporting extensive modding capabilities and multiplayer cooperation. The flexible sandbox design gives players complete freedom to experiment with layouts, automation concepts, and engineering solutions.

Whether constructing efficient assembly systems, designing rail networks, managing logistics robots, or optimizing megafactories, **Factorio** delivers a deeply rewarding automation experience for strategy and simulation enthusiasts on Windows systems.
